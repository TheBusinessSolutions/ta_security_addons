Product Brand

The 'Product Brands' feature elevates product classification within Odoo, enabling the creation, management, and assignment of products to specific brands. This enhancement facilitates easier product searches and grouping, while also supporting comprehensive sales & invoicing reporting by brand.

Quick Start Guide

Step 1

Creating and modifying brands:

  • Navigate to the configuration menu of the Inventory or Sales apps and select the menu option 'Product Brand'
Step 2
  • Create or edit an existing record
  • Define the brand name and set and optionally set a related partner
Step 3
  • Use the smart button on top of a brand record to view the products that are assigned to the brand
Step 4

Assign a brand to a product:

  • From the Sales, Inventory or Purchase app, navigate to the Products menu and select the products option
  • Create or edit an existing record
  • In the 'General Information' tab, locate the 'Brand' field and select the applicable brand
Step 5

Filtering based on a brand:

  • In the product list view, start typing the name of a brand in the search
  • From the menu options in the search select 'Brand'
Step 6

Filtering by brand in the sales order entry catalog view

  • From the sale order product grid access the 'Catalog' view
  • In the sidebar you will see a list of your brands
  • Click on one of the brands to filter the products by that brand
